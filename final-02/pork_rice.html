滷肉飯 BRAISED PORK RICE

The concept of this dish is pretty simple. Baised pork over white rice. It is simple, yet so delicious. The braised pork is made out of pork belly, which is fat and full of collagen. This results in a thick stew that almost sticks to the roof of your mouth. This dish is many Taiwanese people's favourite, since this dish is so accessible and usually very cheap. This is a dish that you really wouldn't have to look for since every shop sells them everywhere.
