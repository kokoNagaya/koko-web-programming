鹹酥雞 XianSuJi

Despite its name, this is often made with not just chicken but all sorts of other ingredients too. Many people add ingredients such as tofu, fish cake, string beans and of course, chicken. These are usually picked out by the customer at the front of the store, than these are sent to the back to be fried by the chef. It is then topped off with salt, white pepper, and Taiwanese basil.
