米漿 RICE MILK

Rice milk is similar to soy milk, in a way that they are both plant based milk. However, different to the rice milk we often get at the supermarkets in the west, rice milk served in Taiwan are often brown and has a thicker consistency. They are usually served warm in a bowl, and actually eaten with a spoon. Many people also often enjoy dipping Shao Bing and You Tiao into the rice milk!
