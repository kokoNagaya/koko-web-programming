豬血糕 PIG BLOOD CAKE

This dish translated probably doesn't sound the most appetising, but this is actually really good. It is made our of (like the name hints) pigs blood and sticky rice. It forms a solid cube which is then usually boiled. This food is super versetaille and can often be eaten on its own or as an ingredient in many different dishses such as hotpot and xiansuji. There are also many different ways to serve this dish, and one common way is to eat this with crushed peanuts and parsely dipped on it.
